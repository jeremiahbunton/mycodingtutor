The Company

We're a small team with a big mission to make computer science less daunting.

Coming from different backgrounds, we understand what it is like to learn how to code. We know it is not easy. We have struggled in many of the same ways you have, and will. What we are building is a community where you can feel comfortable asking the silly questions because only by asking questions about how things work will you learn and grow.

Our mission is to provide you with the support and mentorship you can't get online. We provide custom support tailored around what you learn in the classroom, online, and on your own. We know that even with the best resources in the world, even the best struggle sometimes, and we will be there for you when that time comes. Lets us help you through your struggle. Let us provide that much needed mentorship while you build your skills preparing for your future.

Meet The Team

A small group of experienced and knowledgeable tutors who care about your success!

Jeremiah Bunton

Founder/Computer Programmer

Jeremiah got his B.S. in Computer Science from St Lawrence University and has been programming professionally for the past three years for several large companies. During his time at St Lawrence he spent time as a TA, mentoring students in entry level programming classes. After graduation, Jeremiah took to professional programming but always had a desire to give back to the community that helped him achieve success. During his spare time Jeremiah taught an entry level video game design course for children. As Jeremiah taught this course he realized there wasn't a good place where his students could get help outside of class which led him to start this tutor/mentor program. Jeremiah hopes to reach as many students as possible of all ages and levels to help them see how programming can be simple. Jeremiah believes that programming will help facilitate many skills that will be extremely valuable today, tomorrow, and for the rest of your life.

Tori Russ

Young Learners program director

Tori has her masters in education from Potsdam University in upstate New York and has been teaching for the past two years. Tori has a passion for helping ensure quality education from K-12. Tori uses her expertise to plan course material for different age groups, using her knowledge to provide age appropriate material to keep children engaged and make sure they learn computer programming principles in a fun and interactive way. Tori's passion is seeing kids achieve milestones on an individual basis and she will continue to establish industry education standards in all the courses we provide.
